Pick colors with a native loupe.

teenycolor uses macOS native NSColorSampler to give you a pixel-perfect magnifying loupe. Click anywhere on any screen to grab the exact color under your cursor. The value is copied to your clipboard instantly and a floating HUD confirms the copy.

Every color you pick, remembered.

teenycolor saves your picked colors automatically. Each entry shows a color swatch, the formatted value, and a relative timestamp. History persists between launches and the limit is configurable (20, 50, or 100). Pin your favorites so they stay at the top forever.

Check contrast at a glance.

For every color you pick, teenycolor calculates the WCAG 2.1 contrast ratio against both white and black backgrounds. Color-coded badges show AAA, AA, AA Large, or Fail instantly. No need to open a separate accessibility tool.
